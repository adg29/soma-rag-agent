Re: Acme AI / Acme Inc. - Series A pass

After reviewing the Q1 update and speaking with references, we've decided to pass on the Series A.

Reasons:
1. Valuation: a16z led at a $45M post-money. At $800K ARR that's a 56x revenue multiple. Stretched for this stage.
2. Point72 renewal risk is a real near-term headwind - losing that contract would be a significant ARR hit.
3. Head of Sales gap: founder-led sales at this ARR means the growth model isn't proven yet.

What would change our mind: See them hit $1.5M ARR, hire a sales leader, and close a 3rd enterprise logo. Would revisit at Series B.

Note: Company operates as both "Acme AI" and "Acme Inc." - same company, rebranded in March 2024. Make sure CRM is deduplicated.
